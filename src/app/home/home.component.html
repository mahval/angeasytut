Welcome to Adopt'a Pet

If you are interested in adopting a pet fish or two (or ten!) you have come to the right place.

Our fish are 100% real and top quality. They come straight from our local ponds, and they would all prefer to stay in your fish tank than among predators and plastic waste.

Why fish are awesome

Have you ever owned fish? My husband and our youngest daughter love to sit in front of the aquarium and just watch the shrimp and the fish. It’s very calming and relaxing, and it’s something they can do together.

Still not convinced? How about this:

  1. Fish are great starter pets, especially for children to learn responsibility.
  2. Fish are very calming. Watching a fish swim through the water is very tranquil and relaxing.
  3. You don’t have to worry about a fish making too much noise like a barking dog.
  4. Adding a fish tank to your home space is a great way to add decor.
  5. You could train your fish to do tricks! (We had one Betta that could swim through a hoop for a treat.)
